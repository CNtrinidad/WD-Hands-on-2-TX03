Makoto Misumi

The main protagonist of this series. He is one of the three World Travelers who was summoned to the Goddess' World.


Originally meant to be the only person to be sent to the other world as per the contract between his parents and the Goddess, but because of her own preferences in beauty standards, she abandons and throws Makoto to the edge of her world into the Wasteland, while secretly kidnapping two other people from Earth and sending them to her world as Heroes.
